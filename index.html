Our Chef: Chef Boyardee


Boyardee was born in Piacenza, Italy in 1897, to Giuseppe and Maria Maffi Boiardi. On May 9, 1914, at the age of 16, he arrived at Ellis Island aboard La Lorraine, a ship of French registration.

If he’s not at Bistros, you’ll find him cheering on the Boston Red Sox or spending time with her dog, Meka. he considers Julia Child her hero and her mother to be her greatest inspiration.

Chef Boyardee has been working in the restaurant industry for a very long time and really enjoys the environment Bistros offers.
